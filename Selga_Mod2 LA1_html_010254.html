Leche Flan

Leche Flan is one of the most favorite dessert of every Filipino.

Leche Flan has four primarily ingredients:

  • Egg
  • Condensada
  • Evaporada
  • Sugar

Leche Flan is a dessert made-up of eggs and milk with a soft caramel on top. This delicious dessert is known throughout the world. It is the top dessert in the Philippines.
